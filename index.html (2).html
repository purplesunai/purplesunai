+

How long does setup take?
+
Most clients are fully live within 48 hours. Elite plan clients receive white-glove setup which may take 3–5 business days for full customization.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes. No long-term contracts. Cancel your monthly subscription anytime from your dashboard. We ask for 30 days notice to properly offboard your automations.
What if I'm not in New Orleans?
+
We serve businesses across the United States. The setup process is fully remote — no in-person meetings required.
How does the AI voice receptionist work?
+
Your phone number connects to our AI system. When a call comes in, the AI answers in a natural voice trained on your services, pricing, and FAQs. It books appointments directly into your calendar and sends callers a follow-up text.
Is my business data secure?
+
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell or share your client data. Your dashboard uses enterprise-grade security with two-factor authentication.
