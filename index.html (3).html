Do I need any technical experience?
+
Zero. We handle the entire setup. Fill out a form and we build everything. Most clients are live within 48 hours without touching a single setting.
How long does setup take?
+
48 hours for most plans. Elite plan clients receive white-glove setup which may take 3-5 business days for full custom AI training and configuration.
Will this replace the software I already use?
+
No — and that's intentional. Purple Sun AI sits on top of your existing tools. If you use Towbook, keep it. Vagaro, keep it. Clio, keep it. We add the growth layer those tools don't provide.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes. No long-term contracts. Cancel your monthly subscription anytime from your dashboard. We ask for 30 days notice to properly offboard your automations.
How does the AI voice receptionist work?
+
Your business phone number connects to our AI system (powered by Vapi.ai). When a call comes in, the AI answers in a natural voice trained on your services, pricing, and FAQs. It books appointments directly into your calendar. Customers often don't know it's AI.
What's the AI Web Chat + Google Profile feature?
+
Your AI chat bot is active on your website (via Chatbase) AND on your Google Business Profile (via Google Business Messages connected to GoHighLevel). When someone messages your business from Google Maps, the AI responds instantly, answers their question, and books them — even at midnight.
